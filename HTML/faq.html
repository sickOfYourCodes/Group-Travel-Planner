    Frequently Asked Questions

    Question: How many people can this planner accomodate? Answer: As many as neeeded.

    Question: Will this planner show my individual plans? Answer: Yes, it will show your individual plans, when you log in.

    Question: Will this planner help me budget my daily expenses? Answer: Yes, it will help you budget your daily expenses, as well as show your trip budget for your conveinence.

    Question: How will this planner help me plan my trip? Answer: This planner will help you plan your trip by showing you your budget, what activities your group is planning, and what the group plans are over the course of your trip.
    Question: Will this planner show me what activities are available near me? Answer: No, currently this is something we do not offer on our app, possibly we may add this in the future.
